CalCOFI 2504 Preliminary CTD.csvs

Please note that these data - both CTD AND bottle data are preliminary and made
available for non-publication use. Final CTD.csv and bottle data will be posted
online when they have gone through CalCOFI's data-quality point-checking.
Temperature and salinities may change very little but oxygen, nitrate and 
chlorophyll data may change significantly after post-cruise calibrations and
corrections are applied. These data will be annotated "FinalQC".


2504 had an incident with the Oxygen Rig. A leak within the ship damaged the Oxygen computer which lead to loosing some data. 

Casts 1-8 and do not have bottle oxygen although oxygen samples were taken. 
Casts 11-16 do not have any bottled oxygen and no oxygen samples were taken. 
The Suna Nitrate sensor also had some issues through out the cruise. 
SUNA sensor was removed from casts : 31, 32, 33, 34, 75, 78, 92, 112


Salt and Oxygen data was removed from casts 75 down Primary sensors due to a clog in the pump. 


Currently Oxygen Data is having processing issues. Ox1uM, Ox1uM_CruiseCorr, Ox1uM_StaCorr, Ox2uM, Ox2uM_CruiseCorr, Ox2uM_StaCorr, OxAveuM_StaCorr do not match consistently. Pluse Use Ox1uM and Ox2uM until this is sorted out.
